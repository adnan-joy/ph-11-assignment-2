Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6 Years)

Kindergarten is a preschool educational approach based on playing, singing, drawing, and social interaction from home to school.

Elementary (7-9 Years)

Elementary school is the first stage of formal education for children, usually between the ages of 7 and 9.

Middle (10-16 Years)

Middle school is a school for students in grades 6–8. It focuses on helping students develop social and organizational skills.
